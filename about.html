Know more about our Busking Prince

This 23 years old has this big dream in him to be known as an artist. He also writes his own songs and play a guitar when performing. He was known in his town as "The Busking Prince of Naga City" for he can be seen at nights in the middle of Plaza Rizal entertaining people with his music. Before the pandemic, Renz is a different person. He tried different things to earn money not only for himself but for his family. He put up an online food business which called as "WannaRice". It started well but the pandemic started where restrictions has been stricly implemented. Renz chose to stop temporarily. After that he also tried Apparel business which he named "Wohkul". Then later on, worked as a food server in a well-known restaurant and hotel in the heart of the city. Life has never been easy but Renz, it is his will to keep going and keep believing that someday everything will be worth it.Now, he back to his first love. Music.Together with his guitar , he shares his love for music not only to entertain but to inspire.He really works hard to improve so that he would be able to offter the best "Renz" that he can be.
